Introduction

Exploration vs exploitation

Online decision-making involves a fundamental choice:

  • Exploitation: Make the best decision given current information
  • Exploration: Gather more information

The best long-term strategy may involve short-term sacrifices.

Approaches to exploration

  • Random exploration: Explore random actions (-greedy)
  • Optimism in the Face of Uncertainty: Prefer actions with uncertain values
  • Information State Search: To consider an agent's information as part of its state. Lookahead search incorporating value of information.

Optimistic Initialisation

Initialise to high value (maximum possible reward)

Then act greedily

Encourages systematic exploration early on

But a few unlucky samples can also lock out optimal action

As a consequence, optimistic greedy has linear total regret.
